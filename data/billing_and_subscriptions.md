# Billing & Subscriptions

**Product:** CloudDesk
**Category:** Billing
**Last updated:** 2026-01-28

## Plans
| Plan | Price (per user / month) | Included |
|------|--------------------------|----------|
| Starter | $12 | Core ticketing, 3 integrations |
| Business | $29 | SSO, API access, advanced analytics |
| Enterprise | Custom | SLA, dedicated CSM, audit logs |

Annual billing receives a 20% discount over monthly billing.

## Managing your subscription
- Billing is managed in **Settings → Billing**. Only Workspace Owners and Billing Admins can view or change billing.
- You can upgrade at any time; charges are prorated immediately.
- Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Invoices and payment
- Invoices are issued on the first day of each billing cycle and emailed to all Billing Admins.
- We accept major credit cards and, on Business/Enterprise, ACH and wire transfer.
- Failed payments are retried after 3 and 7 days. After 14 days of non-payment the workspace is set to read-only.

## Common billing questions
**Why was I charged more than expected?** Adding users mid-cycle triggers a prorated charge. Check Settings → Billing → Usage for a per-seat breakdown.

**How do I get a copy of an invoice?** Settings → Billing → Invoices → Download PDF.

## Note on disputes and refunds
Billing disputes, chargebacks, refund eligibility, and tax/VAT questions are **account-sensitive** and are not fully resolvable from self-service documentation. These cases should be handled by a human billing specialist. See the Refund Policy document for eligibility windows.
