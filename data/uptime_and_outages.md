# Uptime, Status & Outages

**Product:** CloudDesk
**Category:** Reliability
**Last updated:** 2026-02-12

## Status page
Real-time service health is published at **status.clouddesk.com**. Subscribe there to receive email/SMS alerts for incidents affecting your region.

## Service regions
CloudDesk runs in three regions: US (us-east), EU (eu-west), and APAC (ap-south). Your workspace is pinned to the region selected at signup; data does not leave that region.

## During an incident
- Check status.clouddesk.com first — most disruptions are tracked there with live updates.
- Incidents are classified as Investigating → Identified → Monitoring → Resolved.
- Degraded performance (slow loads, delayed notifications) is often a partial incident, not a full outage.

## Business impact information
Each incident postmortem includes scope (which features/regions), duration, and root cause. Enterprise customers receive a formal Root Cause Analysis (RCA) within 5 business days of a Sev-1 incident. For questions about how a specific incident affected your workspace's operations or SLA credits, see the Service Level Agreement document.

## Reporting an outage we haven't posted
If you observe an outage not reflected on the status page, report it with your workspace region and the time it began. Widespread issues are prioritized; an unposted outage affecting your workspace should be escalated to support immediately.
