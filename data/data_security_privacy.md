# Data Security & Privacy

**Product:** CloudDesk
**Category:** Security / Compliance
**Last updated:** 2026-02-04

## Encryption
- **In transit:** all traffic uses TLS 1.2+.
- **At rest:** data is encrypted with AES-256.
- Backups are encrypted and stored in the same region as your workspace.

## Compliance
CloudDesk maintains SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications. GDPR and CCPA data processing addenda (DPAs) are available to Business and Enterprise customers on request.

## Data residency
Your workspace data resides in the region chosen at signup (US, EU, or APAC) and is not replicated outside that region except for region-local backups.

## Access controls
- Enforce SSO and 2FA for all members (see respective guides).
- Enterprise audit logs record sign-ins, permission changes, and data exports (Settings → Security → Audit Log).

## Data subject requests (DSR)
Requests to access, export, or delete personal data on behalf of an individual (e.g. GDPR right-to-erasure) are **legal/compliance-sensitive**. Submit them through Settings → Privacy → Data Request. These are reviewed by our compliance team and are not auto-processed.

## Reporting a security concern
Suspected vulnerabilities or data exposure should be reported immediately to security@clouddesk.com. Security incidents are always handled by humans, never closed by automated support.
