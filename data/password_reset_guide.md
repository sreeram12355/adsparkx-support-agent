# Password Reset Guide

**Product:** CloudDesk
**Category:** Account & Access
**Last updated:** 2026-01-12

## Overview
This guide explains how to reset a forgotten CloudDesk password and how to resolve common problems during the reset process.

## Resetting your password
1. Go to the CloudDesk sign-in page and click **Forgot password?**.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account and click **Send reset link**.
3. Open the email titled "Reset your CloudDesk password" (check Spam/Junk if it does not arrive within 5 minutes).
4. Click the **Reset password** button. The link is valid for **30 minutes**.
5. Enter a new password that meets the requirements below, then confirm it.
6. You will be signed out of all active sessions and must sign in again with the new password.

## Password requirements
- Minimum 12 characters.
- At least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one symbol.
- Cannot reuse any of your last 5 passwords.

## Common problems
### The reset email never arrives
- Confirm you entered the exact email used to register.
- Check Spam/Junk and any filtering rules.
- Corporate mail servers may delay delivery by up to 15 minutes.
- If your account uses Single Sign-On (SSO), password reset is managed by your identity provider, not CloudDesk. See the SSO/SAML Configuration guide.

### The reset link says "expired" or "invalid"
- Reset links expire after 30 minutes and can only be used once. Request a new link.
- Clearing your browser cache and cookies resolves most "invalid link" errors.

### Account locked after too many attempts
- After 10 failed sign-in attempts the account is locked for 30 minutes for security.
- The lock clears automatically; you do not need to contact support unless it persists.

## Still stuck?
If you have completed a password reset and a browser cache clear but still cannot sign in, the issue may be an account lock or a provisioning error that requires a human support agent to investigate.
