# Notifications & Alerts

**Product:** CloudDesk
**Category:** Settings
**Last updated:** 2026-01-18

## Notification channels
CloudDesk can notify you via in-app, email, mobile push, and connected chat tools (Slack/Teams).

## Configuring notifications
Go to **Profile → Notifications**. For each event type choose the channels you want:
- Ticket assigned to me
- New comment on my ticket
- Mention (@you)
- SLA breach warning
- Daily digest

## Email digests
- The daily digest summarizes activity from the previous 24 hours and is sent at a time you choose (default 8:00 AM in your timezone).
- Disable the digest by unchecking all digest events.

## Reducing notification noise
- Use **mute** on a specific ticket to stop notifications for just that item.
- Set **working hours** so non-urgent notifications are held until you're online.
- Priority routing: only `High`/`Urgent` tickets trigger push outside working hours.

## Troubleshooting
| Symptom | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Too many emails | Switch from per-event to the daily digest |
| No emails at all | Verify your email is confirmed; check spam; check that events are enabled |
| Push not working | See the Mobile App FAQ |
