# Account Management

**Product:** CloudDesk
**Category:** Account & Access
**Last updated:** 2026-01-15

## Updating your profile
- Edit your name, email, avatar, and timezone in **Settings → Profile**.
- Changing your email sends a confirmation link to the new address; the change is not applied until confirmed.

## Workspace ownership
- Each workspace has exactly one **Owner**. The Owner has full control including billing and deletion.
- To transfer ownership: Settings → Members → ⋯ next to a member → **Transfer ownership**. Both parties receive a confirmation email.

## Closing or deleting an account
- Individual members can leave a workspace from Settings → Members.
- Deleting an entire workspace is permanent and removes all tickets, files, and integrations after a 30-day grace period.
- Workspace deletion is account-sensitive and requires Owner confirmation via email; if data has already been purged it cannot be recovered and requires human support involvement.

## Inactive accounts
- Free trial workspaces are deactivated 14 days after the trial ends.
- Paid workspaces with failed payments become read-only (see Billing & Subscriptions).

## Merging accounts
We do not currently support automatic merging of two workspaces. This requires a manual data migration handled by our support engineering team.
